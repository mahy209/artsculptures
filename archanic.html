Famous Greek Sculptures

Archanic Period

  • Kleobis and Biton (610-580 BCE) Archeological Museum of Delphi
  • Kouros (c.600) Metropolitan Mueum of Art, New York
  • The Strangford Apolio of Anafi (c.600-580) British Museum, London
  • The Diplyon Kouros (c.600) Athens, Kerameikos Museum
  • The Moschophoros or Calf-bearer (c.570) Acropolis Museum, Athens
  • The Anavysos Kouros (c.525) National Archeological Museum of Athens
  • Frieze of the Siphnian Treasury, Delphi (c.525) Archeological Museum, Delphi


To see how Greek designs advanced, compare, for instance, the limestone statue Lady of Auxerre (c.630 BCE,Louvre,Paris), with the "Peplos Kore" (c.530,Acroplis Museum, Athens); compare also, the sounion Kouros (c.600, National Archeological Museum of Athens), with the "Kitios Boy" (490-480,Acropolis Museum, Athens).
